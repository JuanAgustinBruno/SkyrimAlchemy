  • One of the most created multi-purpose potions (and least used), is made by combining Wheat + Giant's Toe + Creep Cluster or River Betty. It makes a potion/poison with three effects and, prior to the Hearthfire add-on, was the single most expensive potion one could make. In addition to being a good way to make money, it is also a great way to increase the Alchemy skill quickly, because the skill increase is directly proportional to the value of the potion created. The effects are: Damage Stamina Regen + Fortify Health + Fortify Carry Weight.
  • A well-known multi-purpose potion is the Creep Cluster + Large Antlers + River Betty. However, the ingredients are more difficult to obtain, as merchants do not stock antlers. The effects are: Damage Stamina Regen + Fortify Carry Weight + Slow.
  • Another useful potion can be made with a Beehive Husk, Bear Claws, and a Hawk Feathers. The result potion will include Fortify Sneak, Fortify Light Armor, and Fortify One-Handed skill. It is a very useful potion for an assassin.
  • A four effect potion made with Dragon's Tongue, Fly Amanita and Scaly Pholiota is notable for being able to be fully cultivated in the garden & greenhouse plots in Hearthfire homes, allowing for a low-effort supply of ingredients to fuel Alchemy levelling.
  • Restore Stamina, Restore Magicka, and Restore Health are the most commonly occurring effects on alchemical ingredients. Combining ingredients to produce multiple effects is therefore easier if one of the said effects is of the restore variety.
  • The most expensive potion discovered to date uses Histcarp, Jazbay Grapes and Salmon Roe. The result grants Water breathing, Fortify Magicka, Fortify Magicka Regeneration, and Restore Stamina. With correct perks and gear, one potion can be worth over 10,000 .
  • Using Mora Tapinella, Scaly Pholiota and Creep Cluster will grant a potion that has at least five effects. Other ingredients that will produce a five-effect serum are:
    • Glow Dust, Glowing Mushroom, Hanging Moss
    • Bear Claws, Eye of Sabre Cat, Hanging Moss
    • Daedra Heart, Eye of Sabre Cat, Silverside Perch
    • Jarrin Root, Human Heart, Blisterwort
    • Dwarven Oil, Lichor, Watcher's Eye.
